Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how you can delete your Numo account and associated data.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the Numo app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

When you delete your account from within the app, your account and associated stored data are permanently removed from our servers.

Request Account Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or would like to submit a manual deletion request, you may contact us directly.

Request Account Deletion

Please send the request from the email address associated with your Numo account to help us verify ownership.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Your account information (email, user ID)
  • Your expense and income records
  • Uploaded receipt images
  • Associated profile data

Certain minimal records may be retained only if required for legal, accounting, or security purposes, in accordance with applicable laws.

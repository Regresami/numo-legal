Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how you can delete your Numo account and associated data.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the Numo app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

When you delete your account from within the app, your account and associated stored data are permanently removed from our servers.

Request Account Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app or would like to submit a manual deletion request, you may contact us directly.

Request Account Deletion

Account deletion requests submitted by email are processed within 7 business days.

Please send the request from the email address associated with your Numo account to help us verify ownership.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon account deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Account information (email address, authentication identifiers, user ID)
  • Profile information (display name, preferences)
  • Expense and income records
  • Uploaded receipt images and related metadata
  • Application usage data associated with your account

We do not retain personal data after account deletion except where required to comply with legal obligations (such as fraud prevention, security, or accounting requirements).

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Numo (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Numo mobile app.

Owner / Controller: Artur Fedichkin (Individual), Ukraine

Contact: numo.app.contact@gmail.com

If you contact support via in-app chat, your messages and attachments are processed to help you with your request.

1) What Numo is (and isn’t)

  • Numo helps you record and analyze personal expenses/spending (and optionally income) and view statistics.
  • Numo does not provide financial advice, investment advice, tax advice, or professional recommendations.

2) Account & authentication

  • You can create an account and sign in using Supabase authentication (email/password and supported identity providers).
  • Email verification is not required.
  • Password reset (“Forgot password”) is performed via email.

3) Data you provide

When you use Numo, you may provide:

  • Account data: email (and authentication identifiers).
  • Financial records you enter or generate: expenses, amounts, currencies, dates, categories, notes, merchant names, etc.
  • Images you choose to upload: e.g., an image of an expense document/photo that you select to help you create an expense entry faster. Images are stored only for your convenience (so you can view and verify them later inside your own account).
  • Support messages: text you send via in-app support chat and optional attachments you choose to add.

4) Camera / Photos

  • Camera: used only if you choose to capture an expense image within the app.
  • Photo Library: used only if you choose an existing image from your device for creating an expense entry.
  • You may choose not to save an expense after previewing the result—in that case, the app is designed not to store that expense record (and associated image) on our servers.

5) Where data is stored

  • Your account and expense data are stored in our backend (Supabase database).
  • Uploaded images and attachments are stored in our backend storage (Supabase Storage; other storage providers may be added later).
  • Your data is account-centric: signing into the same account on another device shows the same data.

6) Who can see your data

  • Only you (the account owner) can access your expenses and uploaded images inside the app.
  • Numo has no public profiles, no social features, and no user-to-user interactions.
  • We do not display your personal data to other users.

7) How we use data

We use your data to:

  • Provide core functionality (store your expenses, show history and statistics).
  • Process optional features you initiate (e.g., extracting expense details from an image you selected).
  • Provide support (respond in in-app chat and/or email).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and operate the service.

8) Sharing & third parties

We use third-party services to operate the app. Depending on the feature you use, your data may be processed by:

  • Supabase (authentication, database, storage, server-side functions).
  • AI processing providers (used only to perform features you initiate, such as extracting expense details from an image). This may include providers such as OpenAI and/or Google/Azure-based OCR/AI services.
  • RevenueCat (subscription and entitlement management).
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (payments and subscription transactions via the stores).
  • Analytics (planned): we may add product analytics later to understand feature usage and improve the app. If added, we will update this Privacy Policy and the store disclosures accordingly.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data with other users.

9) Subscriptions & purchases

  • Numo may offer a Pro subscription and a free trial experience.
  • Purchases are handled via Apple App Store / Google Play, with RevenueCat used for entitlement synchronization.

10) Data retention

  • We keep your data as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide the service.
  • You can delete individual expenses (and associated images where applicable) inside the app.
  • You can delete your entire account inside the app; this is intended to remove your account data from our servers.

11) Account deletion requests

You can delete your account from within the app settings. If you need assistance, contact numo.app.contact@gmail.com. See also: Account Deletion.

12) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be 100% secure.

13) Children

Numo is not intended for children. Please do not use Numo if you are under the minimum age required in your country.

14) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

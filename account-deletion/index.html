Delete Your Numo — Smart Receipt Scanner Account

This page allows users of Numo — Smart Receipt Scanner to request deletion of their Numo account and associated data.

App: Numo — Smart Receipt Scanner

Developer: Artur Fedichkin

Contact: numo.app.contact@gmail.com

Request Account Deletion by Email

You can request deletion of your Numo account even if you cannot access the app or sign in. Send your request using the email button below.

Request Numo Account Deletion

You can also send the request directly to numo.app.contact@gmail.com .

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 7 business days. Processing may take longer if additional account verification or legal retention review is required.

Whenever possible, send the request from the email address linked to your Numo account. If your account was created anonymously or you no longer have access to the linked email, include any information that may help us identify the account.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open Numo — Smart Receipt Scanner.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

This is the primary and fastest way to delete your Numo account and associated data.

What Is Deleted

When your account deletion request is completed, we intend to delete the account data associated with your Numo account from our systems, including:

  • account records stored in our backend, including basic profile information received from authentication providers where available;
  • authentication-related records associated with your Numo account;
  • expense and income records;
  • receipt entries, uploaded receipt images, recognized items, and related metadata;
  • nutrition estimates, nutrient classifications, grocery basket scores, nutrient signals, and related derived records associated with your receipts and account;
  • search history and AI-processing usage logs linked to your account;
  • support chat threads, support messages, and support attachments associated with your account;
  • user settings and user-specific category or subcategory customization records.

What May Be Retained

Some limited information may be retained where reasonably necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or enforcement of limited trial access.

  • We may retain limited device-level anti-abuse or trial-enforcement records, such as records associated with a device identifier used to prevent repeated abuse of free trial access.
  • Billing, subscription, and transaction records may also be retained by Apple, Google Play, RevenueCat, or other parties to the extent required by their systems, contracts, or applicable law.

Important Notes

  • Deleting your Numo account is intended to permanently remove your Numo account data from our systems, except for limited data retained for the reasons described above.
  • If you use Sign in with Apple, Sign in with Google, email and password, or an account later linked to one of those methods, the deletion request applies to the Numo account and its associated data, not only to one login method.
  • If you created an anonymous account and later linked it to another sign-in method, deletion applies to that linked Numo account and associated data.
